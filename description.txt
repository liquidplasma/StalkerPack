S.T.A.L.K.E.R. Content Pack is small mod containing a bunch of items related to the S.T.A.L.K.E.R. series.

Source: [url=https://github.com/liquidplasma/StalkerPack] Github [/url]
Thanks to [url=https://steamcommunity.com/profiles/76561198801238096/] -Faroz- [/url] for lending his [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2937161707] workshop content [/url] to be used on this mod!


The mod includes:
	- 20 ranged weapons
	- 5 food items
	- 2 head pieces belonging to the Stalker and Bandits faction
	- A fully funtional guitar in which you can play campfire songs with
	- A Svarog detector which can help you find useful early game stuff
